FAQ

What is the Utility/Use Case for Wiki Cat?

Wiki Cat started out as a token by Sir. Mapy for the SMC community to teach them how to create meme tokens. Regardless, we believe the use case for Wiki Cat lies in the strength of its community that believes in the social consensus of shared value. This is what has taken the likes of Doge and Shiba Inu to billions of dollars in the market value.

Who owns WikiCat?

Wiki Cat ownership has been renounced. Ownership is collectively owned by the community. There is no CEO, no VC or board members pulling the strings in the background. For this reason, no wallet has more than 5% of the token. Funding or contributions are voluntary and ultimately the community gets to decide the future of the project.
